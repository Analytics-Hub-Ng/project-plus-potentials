About Us

Project + potential is an engineering consultancy firm established in February 2003 and registered by the corporate affairs commission in Nigeria.

We were fully registered as an engineering consulting firm by the Council For Regulation Of Engineering In Nigeria (COREN) in November 2005 with registration no. EF00355. And also registered with the Association of Consulting Engineering in Nigeria (ACEN) in January 2014 with membership no. F287.

Our vision is to produce independent and high-class engineers in our field of engineering and also provide world-class engineering services in our core area of services.

Project + potential was established to provide quality-engineering services as follows:

  • Engineering Consultancy Services
  • Civil/structural engineering services.
  • Mechanical & electrical engineering services.
  • Project/Construction Management.
  • Engineering Procurement.

We engage qualified engineers as staff and also partner with other professionals in the execution of projects.
In order to provide quality services, we work with all relevant codes of practice and run a completely computerized outfit equipped with state of the software (Orion, Robot, cad RC, civil 3D, Prota Structure e.t.c) and a well furnished library. We also have links with institutions locally and internationally via the internet for technical support.
