Who We Are

Project Plus Potentials is an engineering consultancy firm in Nigeria whose vision is to produce independent and high-class engineers in our field of engineering and also provide world-class engineering services in our core area of service.

We engage qualified engineers as staff and also partner with other professionals in the execution of projects.

To provide quality service, we work with all relevant codes of practice and run a completely computerised outfit equipped with state of the art software and a well-furnished library. We also have links with institutions locally and overseas via the internet for technical support.
